FAQ

What is Bitbit? (What can I use it for?)

Bitbit is a social mobile wallet that will let you send to and receive money from friends and family with just a tap. Free from the hassle of long lines, each user has the option to hold your money however you choose, in Peso or Bitcoin, which you can exchange to either currency in the wallet.

How do I load up my account?

You can load your account through bitcoin or request from your Bitbit friends.

How do I send money to my friends or family? How do I receive money?

Once you have synced your social media or mobile contacts to Bitbit, you now have easy access to everyone you know. Either choose from your Bitbit contacts or send to any bitcoin wallet.

How else can I use my wallet?

You can now use Rebit.ph, wherein you can pay any utility, phone, government bills and buy load for your phone. Rebit.ph also allows you to cashout from yout Bitbit wallet using any bank account or pawnshop.

What is the difference of my Peso Wallet from my Bitcoin Wallet?

Just like your BTC wallet, a peso wallet has an address and can receive or store Bitcoin from any platform. The only difference is that if you store your money in your PHP wallet, you are safe from bitcoin volatility.
